Get to know about team

The Placement Cell of our institution, ZillionHire is a dedicated and proactive department that bridges the gap between education and professional success. Committed to enhancing the employability and career prospects of students, the Placement Cell serves as a vital link between students, faculty, and potential employers. Our Placement Cell operates with the primary objective of facilitating smooth and lucrative transitions for students from academia to the corporate world. It is responsible for organizing various activities, workshops, seminars, and training sessions that help students develop essential skills such as communication, problem-solving, teamwork, and leadership. These initiatives not only augment their academic knowledge but also groom them to meet the demands of the competitive job market.
